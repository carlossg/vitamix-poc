Environmental Responsibility
Made in Cleveland, Ohio USA since 1921. Vitamix uses domestic and global sourcing with a commitment to reducing environmental impact. Our factory is designed for efficiency with waste reduction programs in place.
All containers are BPA-free Tritan copolyester. We use recyclable packaging materials and have eliminated unnecessary plastic wrap. No harmful plasticizers or phthalates in any components.
Vitamix blenders are built to last 10-20+ years with proper care. This longevity means fewer blenders in landfills compared to replacing cheap models every 2-3 years.
Blade assemblies and other parts can be replaced individually rather than discarding the entire unit. We offer repair services to extend product life even further.
