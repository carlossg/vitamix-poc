Allergen Safety Guide

Recommended dedicated container strategy for households with severe allergies:

  • Container 1 (marked GREEN): Allergen-free recipes only - never expose to allergens
  • Container 2 (marked RED): Recipes that may contain allergens
  • Use color-coded tape or permanent markers to label clearly
  • Store containers separately to prevent cross-contact
  • All Vitamix containers are BPA-free and made from Tritan copolyester
  • Tritan plastic is non-porous, making it easier to sanitize than some materials
  • Stainless steel blades can withstand high-heat sanitization
  • Silicone gaskets should be inspected regularly for wear and replaced if damaged
